How to Use

Step 1

Download our Android app and sign up.

Step 2

Drive up. Our camera take a picure of your plate.

Step 3

If you are in good standing, the gate opens. Drive in.

Step 4

Leave when you are ready, don't worry we'll bill you.

FAQ

  • What is Smart Parker
    Smart Parker is a parking platform that uses image recogniation to charge patrons. You sign up on the Android app with your license plate and email address. Wehn you show up we take a photo of your license plate. When you leave we take another picture. We use these to determine how long you were in the parking lot and then we bill you. Smart Parker cuts out the need to interact with any outside elements once you arrive at one of our facilities.
  • What is good standing? How to I maintain good standing?
    We will send you an invoice from PayPal. If you pay your invoices ontime you will remain in good standing. You MUST pay your invoice before you can use the service again.
